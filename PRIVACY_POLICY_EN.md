## 3. Personal Data We Collect

### 3.1. Registration and Profile Data
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Profile photo (optional)

### 3.2. Booking Data
- Booked services
- Selected barber/stylist
- Appointment date and time
- Salon location
- Notes or special instructions related to the service

### 3.3. Technical Data
- Device push notification token
- Device type (iOS/Android)
- Operating system version
- App usage analytics

### 3.4. Notifications
- Booking confirmation notifications
- Appointment reminder notifications
- Notifications about booking changes (cancellations/rescheduling)

## 4. How We Use Your Data

### 4.1. Service Delivery
- Create and manage your user profile
- Process and manage bookings
- Coordinate with barbers/stylists
- Send booking confirmations and reminders

### 4.2. Communication
- Send push notifications with important information about bookings
- Respond to your support requests and inquiries
- Inform you about changes to services or policies

### 4.3. Service Improvement
- Analyse app usage to improve functionality and user experience
- Diagnose technical issues and optimise performance

## 5. Legal Bases for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:
- **Contract performance:** to provide the booking services you requested
- **Consent:** for push notifications (you may withdraw consent at any time)
- **Legitimate interests:** to improve services and prevent fraud or misuse

## 6. Data Sharing

### 6.1. Who We Share Data With
- **Staff:** we share booking details (name, phone, appointment time) so staff can provide the service
- **Service providers:** we use third parties for data hosting (Supabase), push notifications (Expo Notifications), and analytics

### 6.2. What We Do Not Do
- We do **not** sell your personal data
- We do **not** share your data with advertisers for marketing purposes
- We do **not** disclose your contact details without your consent

## 7. Data Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest
- Regular backups
- Access controls for authorised personnel only

## 8. Data Retention

### 8.1. Retention Periods
- **Active profiles:** retained while your account remains active
- **Completed bookings:** stored for 3 years after your last appointment (for accounting purposes)
- **Closed profiles:** permanently deleted 30 days after closure

### 8.2. Right to Deletion
- You may request deletion at any time via info@cosanostra.com
- Deletion requests are processed within 30 days unless legal retention requirements apply

## 9. Your GDPR Rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) you have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Rectify** inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Delete** your data
- **Restrict** processing in certain circumstances
- **Portability** of data in a structured, commonly used format
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw consent** for push notifications or other optional features

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@cosanostra.com.

### 10.1. Types of Notifications

### 10.2. Managing Notifications
